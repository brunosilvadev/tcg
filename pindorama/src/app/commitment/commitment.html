Our Commitment

Pindorama was created with the deepest respect for the cultures, traditions, and spiritual symbolism of Indigenous peoples. The stories, ceremonies, and figures represented in these cards belong to living cultures with rich histories that deserve to be honored, not reduced.

This project is intended as an educational tool — a way to spark curiosity and inspire you to learn more about the diverse nations and traditions that have shaped this land for thousands of years. We do not claim authority over any of these traditions, and we recognize that no digital collection can fully capture their depth or meaning.

We are committed to listening. If you are a member of an Indigenous community and have thoughts, corrections, or concerns about how your culture is represented here, we want to hear from you. Your voice matters to us and will directly shape how this project grows.
